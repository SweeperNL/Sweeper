Our Mission

In a world where social hockey teams are overlooked, training shouldn’t be a privilege—it should be for everyone. Too often, these teams lack guidance, repeating the same drills without professional support.

We believe every player, no matter their team’s status, deserves structured training to improve, stay focused, and enjoy the game. Field hockey isn’t just about competition; it’s about community, passion, and growth.

When every team has access to quality training, the sport becomes more than a game—it becomes a lifelong experience.

Improvement

Better every day. Small steps forward create stronger teams and lasting progress. Sweeper provides teams with weekly training plans and blogs on how to learn new tricks to ensure they are always moving forward.

Independency

Great teams don’t sit around waiting for a coach, they become their own. With Sweeper you’re no longer in need for a coach in order keep training. We provide new training schedules by certified coaches and professional athletes that make sure you can train just as well as if you had an external coach.

Guidance

Sweeper provides a clear direction and support for field hockey players, who may not have access or a coach. We are helping teams structure their training and improve their skills even without a coach.

Leadership

We as Sweeper want to stimulate our customers to take the lead in their team. We want to give them the skills they need to lead their team.
