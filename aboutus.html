Our Mission

In a world where social hockey teams are overlooked, training shouldn’t be a privilege—it should be for everyone. Too often, these teams lack guidance, repeating the same drills without professional support.

We believe every player, no matter their team’s status, deserves structured training to improve, stay focused, and enjoy the game. Field hockey isn’t just about competition; it’s about community, passion, and growth.

Improvement

Better every day. Small steps forward create stronger teams and lasting progress. Sweeper provides weekly training plans and blogs to keep you moving forward.

Independency

Great teams don’t wait for a coach. With Sweeper, you become your own. Get schedules from certified coaches and train anytime.

Guidance

Sweeper offers structure and support to teams without a coach, helping them improve together with a clear path forward.

Leadership

We encourage players to step up, lead their team, and gain the confidence to grow — both on and off the pitch.
